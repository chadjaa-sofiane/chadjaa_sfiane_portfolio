# Illustration Replacement Recommendations

## 1) Architecture Mini-Map (Recommended)
A lightweight interactive system map that reflects real engineering work.

- Show nodes such as API, Auth, Database, Queue, Cache, and AI Worker.
- Animate data flow between nodes with subtle motion.
- On hover, show short metrics (latency, uptime, throughput).

Why this is best:
- Communicates senior backend/system design skills quickly.
- Looks technical and intentional without visual clutter.
- Reusable across Home and Projects sections.

## 2) Live Terminal + Deploy Timeline
A stylized terminal and CI/CD flow.

- Simulate build, test, deploy, health-check steps.
- Highlight each step with a calm progress animation.

Why it works:
- Strong DevOps signal.
- Easy to keep clean and professional.

## 3) Systems-in-Motion Abstract Canvas
An abstract technical scene (no character illustration).

- Nodes, links, and packet flow with category toggles (Backend/Infra/AI).
- Keep motion low-amplitude and performance-safe.

Why it works:
- Modern visual identity.
- Scales well with seasonal themes.

## 4) Real Project Spotlight Cards
Use real projects as the hero visual.

- 2-3 featured systems with stack tags and business outcomes.
- Minimal motion and strong readability.

Why it works:
- Highest recruiter clarity.
- Easy to maintain and update over time.
